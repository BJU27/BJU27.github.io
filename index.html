About Me

I grew up in Greenville, SC with two sisters and two brothers. My current church family is at Cornerstone Baptist Church where I have served in the choir, sound booth, and children's church. I have been learning about business and hard work from a very young age, my dad is an entrepreneur and has started a few different companies over the years. We have done paintball, IT, and Embroidery with all of them being successful businesses. From an early age, I worked with my parents in a warehouse getting things off the shelf and doing other small tasks for them. Once we sold that business my family was able to focus more on our IT business. During my junior and senior years of high school, I was able to answer phones for my dad and do level 1 troubleshooting. I currently work at Publix as a grocery clerk where I stock shelves and make sure that the shelves have two of each item pulled to the front. I am excited and willing to learn about how business works and how to be the best worker that I possibly can to make sure that I am an asset instead of a liability to my company.
